About MedInstruct

MedInstruct is a project focused on providing AI-powered, user-friendly instructions for medical devices.

  • BGE-Medico-Model for high-quality semantic search
  • FAISS for fast vector retrieval
  • Groq + Llama 3.1 8B Instant for natural language generation

We collected a dataset of over 13,400 medical devices including names, definitions, and usage instructions.

This tool is designed for nursing students, medical professionals, and educational purposes.
